We are a group of dance enthusiasts with expertise in different forms of dance, teamed up as one in this.
Jaanu is the founder and director of this institution and she specialises in Bharathanatyam, Bollywood and Heels. Haritha the co-founder, specialises in kathak and heels.
The main goal of this academy is to nurture the talents of aspiring dancers and guide them in this field. We also have a lot of other staffs who are well trianed in their respective dance forms.
The academy has taken to online teaching due to the pandemic situation, thus ensuring that no child misses their classes or loose out on their passion.
This academy also trains students in stage hacks, dance makeup, online content creation and social media management.
